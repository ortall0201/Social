## Common Questions

### Q: How long until I rank on Google?
**A**: Typically 2-3 months for long-tail keywords, 6-12 months for competitive terms. Consistency is key!

### Q: Do I need to pay for SEO tools?
**A**: Not initially. Start with free tools (Google Search Console, Google Analytics). Consider paid tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush) after 3-6 months.

### Q: How often should I post?
**A**: Minimum weekly. More is better, but consistency matters more than frequency.

### Q: What if I'm not appearing in AI search (GPT)?
**A**: AI search indexes over time. Focus on creating authoritative, well-structured content. It can take 6-12 months to appear regularly.

### Q: Should I focus on Google or AI search?
**A**: Focus on Google first (90%+ of search traffic). AI search is a bonus that will come naturally with good content.
