## üí° Pro Tips for Maximum Engagement

### 1. **Vary Your Themes**
- Don't post all dark backgrounds in a row
- Mix soft/bold for visual variety on profile grid
- Dark themes = evening, Soft themes = morning

### 2. **Test & Learn**
- Track which captions get highest saves
- Double down on what works
- Archive low-performers after 48 hours

### 3. **Hashtag Strategy**
**Don't overdo it.** Use 3-5 relevant hashtags:
- Primary: `#selflove #confidence #maincharacter`
- Niche: `#healingjourney #softgirl #boundaries`

### 4. **Caption Text (Instagram Caption)**
Keep it minimal. Let the image speak:
- "."
- "Read that again."
- "Needed this today."
- "Tag someone who needs to hear this."

### 5. **Stories Strategy**
- Repost captions that get 100+ saves to Stories
- Add poll: "Did you save this?" (Yes/No)
- Share follower DMs thanking you for the caption

### Why Minimalistic Captions Work:
